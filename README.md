A hilariously fun arcade game born from a viral Pakistani meme. 
Launch a tyre into a Bati — sounds easy, isn't.

🎮 **[Play Now](https://se-khubaib-ktk.github.io/tyre_launcher_pk/)**

---

## 😂 About

You've seen the meme. Now live it.
Charge up your engine, hit the ramp, and pray the tyre lands in the Bati.
Spoiler: it probably won't. 

Built purely for fun and internet glory. 🇵🇰

---

## 🕹️ How to Play

1. **Hold** the mouse button to charge the power meter
2. **Release** to launch the tyre
3. Land it **perfectly into the Bati hole** for 100 points
4. Survive **5 rounds** without losing your mind

---

## ⚠️ Important

- **Desktop only** — Mobile not supported (sorry mobile users, use a proper PC 😄)
- Best on: **Chrome, Firefox, Edge**
- Mouse only — no mobile touch support
